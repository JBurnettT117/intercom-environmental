Our Services

Intercon is uniquely qualified to offer you a wide range of demolition services, including:

  • Heavy Demolition:
    • Building and Structure Removal
    • Site Clearing
    • Full-Scale Commercial Demolition
  • Hard Demolition:
    • Concrete Demolition and Removal
    • Asphalt Removal
  • Soft Demolition:
    • Partial or Complete Building and Structure Demolition
    • Selective Interior or Exterior Demolition
    • Total Building Interior Strip-outs
  • Surface and Elevated Tank Removal

We can also acquire and manage all required permits and documentation including:

  • Texas Department of State Health Services notification
  • Demolition permits from all jurisdictional entities
  • Utility provider location notifications
  • Project closeout documents
