Mold Remediation

The Problem

The health, safety, and liability issues associated with mold have become one of today’s biggest environmental concerns.

The adverse effects of mold exposure and related indoor air quality issues have been thrust into the spotlight in recent years. This has resulted in greatly increased awareness by those affected, and greatly increased liability for those responsible.

The Solution

At Intercon Environmental, Inc. we understand all of these issues, and can help by providing safe, effective, and efficient solutions.

At Intercon we have extensive experience in abatement/remediation. We have used this experience to develop containment construction, and remediation techniques that comply with all state regulations and guidelines.

Our Services

First and foremost, the moisture source must be identified and eliminated. The existing source of active mold growth is then removed along with any suspect porous materials. Next all non-porous materials are thoroughly cleaned and sanitized.

We Perform Mold Remediation for:

  • Commercial buildings
  • Industrial complexes
  • Schools
  • Fire and water damaged buildings
Intercon has a capable staff of professional technicians who are fully certified and specialize in mold remediation.

Our accurate record keeping, strict adherence to protocols, and highly skilled technicians are just a few of the reasons Intercon Environmental, Inc. is the best choice for your mold remediation project.
