About Us

Our History

Intercon Environmental, Inc. began as a premier asbestos abatement contractor in 2004.

Reliable And Reputable

The stellar reputation we developed because of our exceptional performance, reliability, and dependability in asbestos abatement allowed us to quickly expand into mold remediation, and asbestos waste transportation.

The addition of our demolition division makes us the single source for all of your abatement, remediation, and demolition project needs.

Licensed/Certified And Experienced

We are proud to have the following license and certifications:

  • - Texas Department of State Health Services (TDSHS)
  • - Historically Underutilized Business (HUB)
  • - Small Business Enterprise (SBE)
  • - Woman-Owned Business Enterprise (WBE)

Industry Standard Equipment

Capable staff of experienced abatement and demolition project managers, and expansive fleet of state-of-the-art abatement, demolition, transportation equipment, give us the capability to perform multiple large scale abatement and demolition projects simultaneously.

Our Mission Statement

We are committed to providing exemplary asbestos abatement, lead abatement, mold remediation, demolition, and waste transport services that are safe, efficient, and legally compliant, on-time, and at competitive prices.

Simply put – We CAN and WILL get your project done on-time, on-budget, and beyond your expectations.
